DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)
-----------------------------
BuzzCalc helps you track your standard drinks in real time — so you always know how much you've had.

Select your glass size (Middy/Pot, Schooner, or Pint), set the ABV using the slider, and BuzzCalc instantly calculates the number of standard drinks. Tap Add Drink to build up your running total throughout the session.

Features:
• Accurate standard drinks calculation using ethanol density
• Supports Middy/Pot (280ml), Schooner (425ml), and Pint (568ml)
• ABV slider from 2.5% to 10%
• Running total tracker
• Session start time — stamps the date and time your first drink was added
• Session persistence — your total is saved if the app is closed or iOS clears memory, and resets automatically the next day
